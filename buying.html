Looking to Buy

Buying a home in Rhode Island or Massachusetts is easier when the process is clear from the start. Here is the simple path from pre-approval to keys.

Step 1

Get pre-approved

Before touring homes, confirm your budget with a lender so you know your price range and can move quickly when the right home appears.

Step 2

Choose representation

Work with a buyer’s agent who explains the process, protects your interests, and helps structure a strong offer.

Step 3

Build your search plan

Decide on towns, budget comfort zone, property type, and must-haves so your search stays focused.

Step 4

Tour homes carefully

Look beyond the first impression and evaluate condition, layout, location, resale factors, and comparable sales.

Step 5

Write and negotiate the offer

Your offer may include price, financing, inspections, deposit, and closing date. Terms matter, not just price.

Step 6

Inspections to closing

After inspections, appraisal, final loan approval, and the final walkthrough, you sign closing documents and get the keys.
