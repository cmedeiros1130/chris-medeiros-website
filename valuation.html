AVM vs. Comps vs. Appraisal

An AVM is an automated valuation model. Sites like Zillow, Realtor.com, Redfin, RPR, and Homes.com use public data and algorithms to estimate value. These tools can be helpful, but they do not always account for condition, upgrades, layout, neighborhood demand, or current buyer activity.

A real CMA compares your home against recent nearby sales and active competition. An appraisal is a formal opinion of value typically ordered by a lender.
