My Seller Roadmap

A Clear Plan From Value to Closing

1

Understand Your Value

We look at recent comparable sales, property condition, updates, location, and current demand to create a realistic pricing strategy.

2

Prepare the Property

Decluttering, cleaning, small repairs, curb appeal, and presentation can help your home stand out and attract stronger buyers.

3

Launch the Marketing

Your property gets exposure through MLS, online platforms, social media, buyer networks, and strategic promotion.

4

Review Offers Carefully

The highest offer is not always the best offer. Financing, inspections, closing date, deposits, and contingencies all matter.

5

Navigate Inspections

I help you stay ahead of inspection requests, buyer concerns, timelines, and any negotiation points that come up.

6

Close With Confidence

From contract to closing, I help coordinate the details so you know what to expect before the keys are handed over.
